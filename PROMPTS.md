# AI Reply Prompts

## First Reply Prompt

Write a short friendly reply to a new coaching lead.
Use their name when available.
Keep it under 40 words.
Do NOT make big promises.
Tone: warm, polite, simple.
Goal: encourage a reply.

---

## Follow-Up Prompt

Write a gentle follow-up message.
Keep it under 35 words.
Do NOT pressure.
Tone: calm, kind, supportive.
