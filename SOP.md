# SOP — Client Delivery Guide 

This document explains how to install and deliver the AI Lead Autoresponder System.

---

## Step 1 — Collect Client Information

Ask the client:

1. Where do their leads come from?
2. Do they already use Google Sheets?
3. What tone do they want in replies?

---

## Step 2 — Create Google Sheet

Columns:

Name
Email
Status
Message
Reply

---

## Step 3 — Build Automation

Trigger:
New or updated row in Google Sheet

Condition:
If Status = New or Follow-up

Action:
Generate AI reply using stored prompts

Write reply back to sheet (Reply column)

---

## Step 4 — Testing

1. Add test lead
2. Check AI reply
3. Adjust tone if needed
4. Review with client

---

## Step 5 — Launch

1. Turn automation ON
2. Provide short demo
3. Answer simple questions
4. Share help doc

---

## Step 6 — Support

Included:

✓ Fixing automation if it breaks
✓ Updating replies
✓ Optimizing workflow

---

## Time Required

Setup time: 1–2 hours

---

## Client Benefit

✓ Saves time
✓ Builds relationships
✓ Books more calls
✓ No tech stress
